Visual & Apparel Direction

2025

Objective

To create a bold and modern brand identity for Northbase, reflecting its strength, precision, and premium positioning in the outdoor and performance gear market. The goal was to design a visual system that feels timeless yet contemporary, appealing to a discerning, adventure-driven audience.

What I've done

1. Brand strategy & positioning
2. Logo design & visual identity system
3. Typography & color palette development
4. Packaging design for core product line
5. Brand guidelines for consistent application
6. Lifestyle & product photography direction

Visual & Apparel Direction

2025

Creative Process
‍

1. Research: I explored Northbase’s core values, market positioning, competitors, and target audience to fully understand the brand’s DNA and long-term vision.

‍
2. Concept Development: Based on the research, I sketched multiple logo and identity directions that balanced modern minimalism with a sense of durability and trust.

‍
3. Digital Design: Selected concepts were developed in Adobe Illustrator, focusing on precision, scalability, and versatility across applications.

‍
4. Refinement: The chosen design was refined through feedback rounds, fine-tuning proportions, typography, and visual balance for maximum impact.

‍
5. Finalization: Delivered a complete brand package — logo files in multiple formats, brand guidelines, and mockups to ensure seamless implementation across digital and print.

Results

‍
The new identity gave Northbase a confident, premium presence in the outdoor and performance market. The design reinforced brand recognition, elevated its appeal to the target audience, and provided a consistent visual framework for all brand touchpoints.

Conclusion

‍
Through a strategic and collaborative approach, I created a bold and timeless brand identity for Northbase — one that communicates strength, precision, and trust, while remaining adaptable for future growth.
